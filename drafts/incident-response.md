# Incident Response Draft

Draft guidance for public documentation.

## Credential exposure checklist

- Identify whether the leaked credential belongs to a current environment.
- Rotate accounts used for smoke tests.
- Remove temporary QA account references before publishing archives.
- Confirm no crew portal password was reused in public repositories.
